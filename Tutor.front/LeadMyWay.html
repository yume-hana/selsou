About Lead My Way

Lead My Way is a platform dedicated to supervising first-year university students exclusively. Here, students can ask questions to their tutor and receive personalized answers to guide them.

How It Works

Students can post questions for tutors and get answers directly on the platform. Additionally, they can schedule face-to-face meetings with their tutor to receive personalized support (not online).
